About Me

Hello! I'm Rafael Longhi, a 17-year-old developer with a passion for technology and innovation. I've been coding since I was 10, starting with simple scripts and gradually expanding my skills across multiple programming languages such as Python, Java, JavaScript, Lua, and C#. My journey in the tech world has been both challenging and rewarding, and I'm always eager to learn and grow.

One of the highlights of my experience was participating in the OBR (Olimpíada Brasileira de Robótica), where I secured 3rd place in the regional competition and 6th place in the state-level competition. This experience not only honed my technical skills but also taught me the value of teamwork, problem-solving, and perseverance.

Currently, my main focus is on preparing for the ENEM and working on an exciting project—Aegis, a game that combines my love for coding and creativity. I'm always exploring new technologies and working on personal projects that challenge my skills and fuel my passion.

Looking ahead, I aspire to study Computer Engineering at UTFPR and join the Apple Developer Academy. I believe that these opportunities will allow me to deepen my knowledge, connect with like-minded individuals, and contribute to innovative projects that can make a difference.

Outside of coding, I have a strong interest in video games and music, which not only provide me with relaxation but also inspire my work as a developer. Whether it's exploring new game mechanics or composing music, I find these hobbies to be a great source of creativity and motivation.

I'm excited about the future and the possibilities it holds, and I'm always open to new opportunities and collaborations. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
